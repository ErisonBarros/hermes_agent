### About me

**I'm a PhD in Civil Engineering in the concentration area: Transportation and Urban Infrastructure Management. Master in Geodetic Sciences and Geoinformation Technologies Cartographer Engineer, Building Technician developer and Geospatial data science Research  and Topograph and Adjustment of Observations.**

---
<p align="justify">Ph.D. in Civil Engineering from the Federal University of Pernambuco (UFPE), with a concentration in Transportation and Urban Infrastructure Management (2022). Certified Drone Pilot - Class 3. Master’s degree in Geodetic Sciences and Geoinformation Technologies from the Federal University of Pernambuco (2011). Bachelor's degree in Cartographic Engineering from the Federal University of Pernambuco (2007). Technical high school diploma in Building Construction from CEFET-PE (currently IFPE). Postgraduate specialization in Engineering Auditing, Evaluation, and Expert Analysis (2014) from IPOG.Currently serves as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Cartographic Engineering at UFPE/CTG/DECART. Internship Coordinator of the Department of Cartographic Engineering, Coordinator of the Topography Laboratory (Latop), and Coordinator of the Topographic Automation Laboratory (LABAT).Presently, President of the Pernambuco Regional Chapter of the Brazilian Association of Cartographic Engineers and Surveyors (ABECA-PE). Alternate Council Member of the Superior Council of the Federal Institute of Education, Science, and Technology of Pernambuco (IFPE).
Previously served as the Inspector Coordinator of the Regional Council of Engineering and Agronomy of Pernambuco (CREA-PE) at the Carpina-PE Office (2009-2017). Former Vice-Coordinator and Vice-Head of the Department of Cartographic Engineering at UFPE. Also worked as a Substitute Professor in the Department of Cartographic Engineering at UFPE. Former Land Reform and Development Technician at the National Institute for Colonization and Agrarian Reform (INCRA), Pernambuco Regional Office (SR-03/PE). 
Served as Vice President of the Rural Property Certification Committee of the Pernambuco Regional Superintendence (SR-03/PE). Participated as a member of the Testing and Approval Team for the Land Management System (SIGEF). Member of the Working Group for the Automated Rural Property Certification System and the Working Group on Rural Land Regularization(TJPE).</p>
